Howl's Moving Castle

Howl's Moving Castle is a Japanese animated fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. It is based on the novel of the same name by Diana Wynne Jones.

The story follows a young girl named Sophie who is cursed by a witch and transformed into an old woman. She seeks the help of a wizard named Howl, who lives in a magical moving castle, to break the curse.

The film was released in 2004 and received critical acclaim for its animation, storytelling, and music.
